Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how YomuLAB collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect and store the following user data:

  • Username – Required for account identification.
  • Sign-up date – Used for administrative purposes.
  • Profile picture – Optional and user-uploaded.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously. Passwords are securely hashed using Bcrypt, and we do not store plaintext passwords. Our database is protected using industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing access to the YomuLAB platform.
  • Personalizing your experience with profile pictures.
  • Ensuring account security and authentication.

5. Third-Party Sharing

YomuLAB does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your information is used solely for the functionality of our platform.

6. User Control & Deletion

You may request to delete your account and all associated data at any time. Contact us through our support page to request account deletion.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Continued use of YomuLAB after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have any privacy-related concerns, reach out to us via our contact page.
